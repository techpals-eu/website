Software Engineer - Go

We are looking for a passionate Software Engineer with a strong technical background in Go and great problem solving skills to join us. The candidate will work in a fast pacing product with focus on developing new features.

Requirements
  • At least 3 years of professional experience in building production-grade, cloud-native, web based systems, using golang, C#, Java, or other languages.
  • Very good understanding of agile development and CI/CD principles and practices.
  • Experience with containerization.
  • Familiarity with SQL (preferably Postgre SQL).
  • Familiarity with cloud computing platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Strong written communication skills in English.

Extra credits
  • Previous working experience with Gin-Gonic.
  • Product development experience.
  • Experience working with a remote team.
  • Familiarity with Terraform.
