Fullstack Developer

We are looking for a passionate Fullstack Developer with a strong technical background in .NET and Angular, with great problem solving skills to join us. The candidate will work in a fast pacing product with focus on developing new features.

Requirements
  • At least 2 years of professional experience in building production-grade, web based systems, using C#, ASP.NET, .NET Core.
  • Previous working experience with Angular
  • Very good understanding of agile development.
  • Familiarity with SQL (preferably Microsoft SQL).
  • Familiarity with cloud computing platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Strong written communication skills in English.

Extra credits
  • Familiarity with container technologies (Docker/Kubernetes)
  • Product development experience.
  • Experience working with a remote team.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD tools (Azure DevOps, CircleCI).
